Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about pricing and plans

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription whenever you want. No questions asked, no hidden fees.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes! Annual plans include 20% savings compared to monthly billing. Use the toggle above to see annual pricing.

Is my data secure?

We use end-to-end encryption for all meetings. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're GDPR and SOC2 compliant.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. Invoicing available for Business plans.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes are prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Do you have a free trial?

Yes! Team plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You can cancel anytime during the trial.
